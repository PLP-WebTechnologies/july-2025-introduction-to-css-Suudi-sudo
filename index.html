Our Story

Sweet Dreams Bakery has been serving the community with fresh, handcrafted baked goods since 2023. This website demonstrates CSS fundamentals through our bakery's online presence.

The Box Model is fundamental to CSS layout, just like quality ingredients are fundamental to great baking. Every element is essentially a box with content, padding, border, and margin areas that can be styled independently.
